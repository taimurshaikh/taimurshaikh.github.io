Dubai Computing Community (Feb. 2021-Present)

The DCC is an informal, entirely student-led CS-based community server targeted at UAE students, but anyone is welcome to join. I lead this initiative as a co-founder and Head. My responsibilites include: delegating tasks to the rest of the leadership team, assisting in event-planning, marketing and social media managing where necessary, and devising and executing initiatives for community engagement. Right now, we're at approx. 300 members, and it's amazing to see how far this diverse, collaborative group of people has come.
