Compile (2021)

I was looking for a way to combine my interests, and I was noticing a lot of people were using AI to make music in one way or another. Inspired, I decided to try it out myself. I wrote a genetic algorithm that can generate melodies, and used it to make an album of lofi music. You can find out more about the algorithm in the Coding section. The two best parts of this album, in my opinion, are the cover art and the song titles. The fingerprint design on the cover art is a fragment of the actual code used in the algorithm, designed by one of my insanely talented friends. Each track is named after a CS concept, and most of the time, they're puns based on the characteristics of the song. Computer scientists, see if you can figure them out!

Cocoon (2020)

During the Dubai quarantine, I was pretty much alone with my thoughts for five months. Turns out that's a pretty good environment for writing an album. Cocoon is about the craziest year of my life, as well as a celebration of my of my growth as a musician. The entire album was recorded and produced by yours truly. After working pretty much non-stop for the majority of the quarantine, this is what I came up with. Enjoy!
