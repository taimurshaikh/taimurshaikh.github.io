Other things I do.

Here are some miscellaneous things I've made/am part of that I enjoy.

LED Music Visualizer (2021)

After getting an Arduino microcontroller, I was looking for a project that would really solidify my skills with electronics. One LED Strip, a digital microphone and hours of trouble shooting later, I made this cool LED visualizer, which sends sound waves down an LED strip depending on the volume of music it picks up. I set it up on my desk and I'm really pleased with the results!


Boxing (2018-Present)

I love boxing. Not only is it a great full-body workout, but it also trains your mind to be focused and sharp. I participated in my gym's quarterly Fight Night in October 2019 and won my fight in Round 1 by knockout!

half_byte (Feb. 2021-Present)

Myself and three of my CS classmates decided to start a podcast where we just talked about everything CS.
